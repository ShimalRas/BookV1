3.1 NUMBERED ARTIFACT #27: "ECLIPSE TYRANT"
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Type: Legendary Sword (One-of-a-Kind)
Status: Bound – Sole Wielder: Alok

Description:
Forged in a forgotten era where day and night, storm and silence bowed to one sovereignty, Eclipse Tyrant answers only to those whose spirit embraces the spectrum of elements. Its jet-black blade is woven through with shifting veins of frost, flame, thunder, water, metal, light, and darkness—transforming an exceptional wielder into an incarnation of nature's full might. It is said its powers deepen as its bearer grows, eclipsing all rivals.

BASE EFFECTS:
• Elemental Amplification: All elemental magic and skills are amplified by 140% while wielding Eclipse Tyrant.
  - Current elements damage: 200% + (20% mana vein boost)

• Elemental Adaptation: Wielder gains 20% resistance to all elemental damage, passively scaling with mastery.
  - Current fire resistance: 60%
  - Current lightning resistance: 60%
  - Current frost resistance: 50%
  - Current wind resistance: 35%

ACTIVE SKILLS:

• Sovereign Cascade:
  Unleash a devastating wave of three or more elemental energies in a sweeping arc. Each element manifests as a separate strike, chaining into one another for overwhelming damage and status effects.

• Aspect Shift:
  Change the sword's "attunement" to any of the wielder's elemental affinities, causing Eclipse Tyrant to transform its color and properties—e.g., a flaming blade burns enemies, a frost blade chills, a light blade radiates holy damage, and so on. Shifting is instantaneous and can occur once per turn.

• Primal Aegis:
  Briefly summon a rotating barrier of elemental shields that reduce incoming elemental damage by 70% for up to 10 seconds. Any absorbed energy empowers the next attack.

ULTIMATE ABILITIES (1/day each):

• Elemental King's Judgement:
  Channel all available elemental affinities into a single cataclysmic attack. Eclipse Tyrant erupts in a cascade of combined elemental fury, devastating foes in an area and reshaping the battlefield to reflect the elements wielded.

• Blizzard Thunderfirestorm:
  Summon a supernatural cataclysm: from the heavens, a storm of razor-sharp frost, wild wind blades, crimson lightning bolts, and blazing fire meteors descends, targeting all foes in a wide radius. Each strike inflicts severe elemental damage and may weaken enemy resistances. Cannot be interrupted and leaves behind a lingering elemental field for several seconds.

Note: This sword is bound to Alok and cannot be held by anyone other than him.

4.4 WORLD STRUCTURE & INTERACTIONS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

PANTHEONS GOVERN FRAGMENTS:
For every cosmic concept (like Death, Life, Thunder, War), most pantheons possess a representative, but only Azrakel and Seraphae embody the primal essence above all.

GODLY HIERARCHY:
Supreme (Azrakel/Seraphae) → Major Pantheon Deities (e.g., Hades, Ra, Odin, Shiva) → Minor Deities & Spirits

DEMONIC COUNTERBALANCE:
Demons and Devils exploit unguarded realms, forging so-called "hells," but are as likely to aid as to destroy—depending on their contracts, chaos, or ambition.

THE TOWER:
A neutral testing ground that attracts the gaze of all—god, demon, and mortal. Its trials call forth the interest and wagers of every divine/demonic power.

                          6. CRAFTING MATERIALS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
6.1 COMMON ORES
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

• Iron Ore: Standard, durable metal used for weapons, tools, and construction.
• Copper Ore: Malleable, used for wiring, enchantment bases, or alloying.
• Tin Ore: Soft, used mainly for alloys such as bronze.
• Coal: Essential for forges, and combining heat magic with metalwork.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
6.2 UNCOMMON ORES
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

• Bronzite Ore: A copper/tin alloy ore that resists corrosion, often has magical conductivity.
• Silverite: Slightly magical and very conductive; used for charms, magical weapon linings, or as a base for enchanting.
• Ferrite Crystal: Iron with crystalline growths, useful for strengthening weapons or as magic amplifiers.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
6.3 RARE ORES
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

• Mithril: Lightweight, silvery, extremely strong and magically receptive. Ideal for armor and magical items.
• Arcanite: Blue-green ore found near ley lines, amplifies spells and stores mana.
• Blood Iron: Blood-red veined iron, stronger and more menacing; sometimes bonded with the blood of monsters, making it harder and giving it minor lifesteal properties.
• Froststeel: Pale-blue ore found in cold regions; imbues forged weapons with frost magic or chilling properties.
• Obsidite: Volcanic glass infused with elemental fire or earth, incredibly sharp but brittle.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
6.4 VERY RARE / EXOTIC ORES
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

• Dragonbone Ore: Fossilized dragon bones laced with minerals; nearly unbreakable, can hold enchantments especially well.
• Voidsilver: Iridescent, shadowy silver that resists magic and curses; prized by rogue mages and monster hunters.
• Aurichalcum: Sun-gold metal, blazes bright under enchantment, sometimes used for holy or radiant weapons and armor.
• Stardust Ore: Rare meteorite metal that glimmers with inner light; absorbs and reflects magic, sometimes used in legendary artifacts.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
6.5 LEGENDARY / MYTHIC ORES
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

• Adamantium: Nearly unbreakable, black-green metal. Alok already possesses a lump—regarded as the stuff of legend; famed for peerless strength and magic resistance.
• Eternium: Mytho-magical, silvery metal said to never tarnish or age; rumored to enhance the user's life force and magical connection.
• Netherite: Deep purple-black ore, exuding a faint pulse. Repels most common enchantments, favored by demon-slayers and artifact makers.
• Celestium: Prismatic metal found only in fallen starmetal deposits or ancient ruins; said to harmonize with the wielder's soul, shifting properties based on their intent.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
6.6 UTILITY MINERALS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

• Magestone: Crystal clusters that store and channel mana; vital for runecrafting, enchanting, magic tech.
• Blastite: Volatile orange-red ore, sometimes used for traps, bombs, or as a power source for magical machinery.
• Luminite: Naturally glowing white-gold mineral, used for magical lanterns, light-enchanted weapons, or visual effects in armor.
