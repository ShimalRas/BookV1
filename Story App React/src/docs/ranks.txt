4. TOWER REALMS STRUCTURE
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- **Floors 1–10: Beginning Realms / Mortal Gateway**
    - Theme: The first steps; trials focused on adaptation, basic magic, foundational combat, teamwork, and courage.
    - Enemies: Goblins, orcs, elemental beasts, beginner undead.
    - Bosses: Mana-wrought golems, spirit knights, or first "tower guardian" types.
    - Atmosphere: Labyrinths, training halls, natural caverns, ancient ruins.

- **Floors 11–20: Wild Realms / Elemental Domains**
    - Theme: Nature unleashed—players face powerful natural forces and must adapt to ever-changing environments.
    - Enemies: Elemental spirits, fae, wild beasts, plant monsters, water spirits.
    - Bosses: Elemental guardians (fire, water, earth, air), giant beasts, fae lords.
    - Atmosphere: Shifting forests, burning fields, flooded caves, floating islands.

- **Floors 21–30: Civilized Realms / Lost Kingdoms**
    - Theme: Test of leadership, diplomacy, or strategy—ancient lost empires or civilizations inhabited by intelligent monsters and spirits.
    - Enemies: Armored undead, spectral knights, monstrous "court" members, ancient automatons.
    - Bosses: King or Queen-ruled monster courts, haunted pharaohs, wraith generals.
    - Atmosphere: Ruined palaces, haunted castles, crumbling cities, clockwork fortresses.

- **Floors 31–40: Labyrinth Realms / Trickster Halls**
    - Theme: Puzzles, illusions, trickster magic, and psychological challenges dominate; combat is as much about wit as strength.
    - Enemies: Mimics, doppelgangers, shapechangers, trickster spirits.
    - Bosses: Tower trickster, illusionist overlords, fear mages.
    - Atmosphere: Shifting mazes, halls of mirrors, reality-warped passages.

- **Floors 41–50: War Realms / Battlefield Domains**
    - Theme: Epic battles; large-scale fights, faction wars, and "raid battle" challenges. Coordination/training is key.
    - Enemies: War golems, disciplined armies, siege beasts, legendary heroes as opponents.
    - Bosses: War generals, ancient war machines, grand champions.
    - Atmosphere: Ravaged fields, siege castles, moving warcamps.

- **Floors 51–60: Mythic Realms / Dreaming Lands**
    - Theme: Legendary monsters and magical landscapes—challenges based on heroic deeds, riddles, or legendary tasks.
    - Enemies: Mythic beasts, enchanted guardians, dream creatures.
    - Bosses: Dragon lords, sphinxes, legendary monsters.
    - Atmosphere: Surreal dreamscapes, world trees, floating sanctuaries.

- **Floors 61–80: Astral/Ethereal Realms**
    - Theme: Reality blurs—gravity, time, and logic no longer hold sway. Tests of soul, mind, and will.
    - Enemies: Phantasmal entities, mind flayers, astral marauders, ancient spirits.
    - Bosses: Astral titans, soul-collectors, legendary liches.
    - Atmosphere: Endless sky, shifting constellations, reality-warped corridors, landscape in flux.

- **Floors 81–89: Desolation Realms / Apocalypse Levels**
    - Theme: Facing ruin and despair—destroyed worlds, calamity beasts, and end-of-all-trials.
    - Enemies: Cataclysmic monsters, world-enders, void entities.
    - Bosses: Apocalypse dragons, void apparitions, harbingers of the end.
    - Atmosphere: Collapsing cities, crumbling void, time-frozen catastrophes.

- **Floors 90–100: Heavenly Realms / Thrones of Ascension**
    - Theme: Divine and infernal domains; seat of gods, demons, and supreme entities. Only the mightiest may challenge these floors.
    - Enemies: Archangels, demon princes, celestial armies, godspawned beast.
    - Bosses: Throne guardians, demon lords, minor deities, trials set by the Tower’s own will.
    - Atmosphere: Marble palaces, shining heavens, infernal fortresses, celestial battlefields, halls of judgment.

LEVELING AND TIERS (CANON CLARIFIED VERSION)
===========================================

DEFINITIONS (IMPORTANT)
-----------------------
Stats:
- STR = Strength
- AGI = Agility
- VIT = Vitality
- INT = Intelligence

PHY (Physical Stats):
- "PHY" is a category meaning STR + AGI + VIT (used only in growth rules and shorthand notes).

Attribute Gain Per Level (Rank Table):
- The "Attribute Gain Per Level" values in Section (3) are a POINT POOL.
- You gain that many (Attribute Points) each level, then you distribute them into STR/AGI/VIT/INT as you choose,
  subject to your build/species/class biases and caps.

Tier Progression Benchmarks (Section 4):
- In Section (4), "Physical" is NOT the same thing as PHY in Section (2).
- "Physical" in Section (4) is a shorthand benchmark for (STR + AGI) only.
- VIT is listed separately in Section (4) because it scales faster than STR/AGI in your setting.

2) BASE STAT GAIN PER LEVEL (SIMPLE MODEL; CATEGORY SHORTHAND)
--------------------------------------------------------------
This section is a HIGH-LEVEL shorthand for typical growth tendencies by major rank bands.
It describes "baseline leaning" (how builds usually allocate points), not mandatory allocation.

- E to A ranks:
  - Typical allocation pattern per level: +1 to (one of STR/AGI/VIT) and +2 to INT
- S to Mythic-2:
  - Typical allocation pattern per level: +3 to Physical (distributed across STR/AGI/VIT) and +5 to INT
- Demi to Divine:
  - Typical allocation pattern per level: +5 to Physical (distributed across STR/AGI/VIT) and +10 to INT

Notes:
- PHY here means the physical-stat CATEGORY (STR, AGI, VIT).
- Exact point allocation is governed by Section (3): Rank Attribute Gain Table (point pool).
